Perceptron

  • Tell the perceptrons which color dots are the most common.
  • The numbers beneath them will tell you.
  • If you lie to the perceptrons, they will die.
  • Also, if they are stupid, they may die anyway.
  • Train them for a while, if you please.
  • Hold S to mutate a perceptron, the longer you hold it, the more will mutate, and more mutations will accumulate.
  • Press E to see the best weighting so far (this will ruin the genepool).
  • Hold Z to answer honestly automatically, for quick training.
